Celebrate Your Special Moments With Us!

Pahadi Thali 🍛

A wholesome platter featuring the rich flavors of the mountains! Includes Pahadi dal, local vegetable sabji, Dubka, Madwa Roti (4), Pahadi Raita, Chutney, Salad, Rice, and a sweet dish. A must-try for those who want an authentic taste of the hills!

Bonfire BBQ Chicken 🔥🍗

Perfect for chilly evenings, this charcoal-grilled chicken is marinated in a flavorful blend of spices and herbs, giving it a juicy and smoky taste. Best enjoyed with friends, music, and a cozy bonfire!

Special Masala Dosa 🥞

A South Indian delight with a crispy golden dosa, stuffed with a spiced potato filling and served with coconut chutney and sambhar. A perfect pick for those craving comfort food with a crunch!

Chilli Chicken 🌶️🍗

A sizzling Indo-Chinese favorite with crispy chicken tossed in a spicy, tangy sauce with capsicum and onions. A crowd favorite that pairs well with fried rice or noodles!

Cold Coffee with Ice Cream ☕🍦

A refreshing treat for coffee lovers! A rich, creamy blend of chilled coffee topped with a scoop of vanilla ice cream, making it the perfect cooling and energizing drink for any time of the day.
